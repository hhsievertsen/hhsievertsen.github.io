I am a senior lecturer at the Department of Economics, University of Bristol.
I am also affiliated with VIVE in Copenhagen. I use data to study topics related to inequality with a special interest in education, health and the labour market. My CV is available here.

  • Mar 31, 2020: In collaboration with Simon Burgess I contributed to IZA World of Labor on "The long-term consequences of missing a term of school".

  • Mar 23, 2020: Together with Jonas Lau-Jensen Hirani and Miriam Wüst I published the working paper "The Timing of Early Interventions and Children's Health" .

  • Nov 26, 2019: My paper with Claus Thustrup Kreiner was featured by the Danish National Research Foundation "New study from CEBI: Parents’ birth weight influences children’s performance in school" [Dansk].
